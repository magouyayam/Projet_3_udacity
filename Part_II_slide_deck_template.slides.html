Distribution of the Annual Percentage Rate (APR)¶

The distribution of the annual percentage rate in the data set shows a very wide range of values from about 0 to 0.51 with many peaks, the highest of which is about 0.35

Distribution of the initial loan amount¶

The initial loan amount is the amount of the loan without the interest rates. They have an asymmetrical distribution tilted to the right with values that are too large on the left. with values up to 35000$.

Loan duration distribution¶

Most loan applicants choose between 12 and 60 months to repay their loan. And most people opt for 36 months.

Distribution ProsperRating (Alpha)¶

The bank assigns scores to each loan from AA to HR and we see that class C is the modal class

Bar Chart of Custom Score¶

For the custom score the bank assigns them according to the history. 4 is the modal class

Initial amount as a function of duration¶

For the initial amount of the loan we found that the more the duration increases the higher the amount of the loan, so these two variables have a positive correlation, the increase of one leads to the increase of the other

APR as a function of ProsperRating and loan term¶

The lowest rates correspond to ProsperRation values of AA followed by A and we can see that in these two modalities the rate increases with the duration so the relationship that we have highlighted above is confirmed by this graph
